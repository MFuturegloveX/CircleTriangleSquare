Assembly

This is a documentation archival of assembly, in a chronological order.

Shape mods used to be called Assembly, There is no head shape mods, And there are only 3 members of assembly at maximum.
Every month, there is an election for each shape splited into two form.
In the first form, you would sign up as an candidate
In the start the format was:

  1. Choice of leadership, pick between
    • Assembly spot
    or
    • Sole leadership (self explanatory)
  2. Faction (optional)
    • None
    • Angle
    • Vertex
    • Corner
    • Point
  3. Your speech:
In the second form, you would select which candidate to be assembly
Like
  1. candidate a (most perfered)
  2. candidate b
  3. candidate c (least perfered)
And add more/less depending the case.
And candidate cannot vote.

Edge cases

If there were 3 candidate and they all pick assembly, No vote is needed.
But if some of them choose to be sole leader, If the member running for sole leader wins 1st place, they will be the leader. If a member running for an assembly wins 1st, the top 3 members running for assembly will win.
If a tie happend it may result assembly too.

Changes

Debate:In around August 2024 an new option was added for leadership form as the third question, "Would you participate in a debate? YES/NO" it pushed speech as the fourth option.
As far as im aware, there were no debate and the option was removed in october.

Parliament: (None/Representative/Free/Supreme), Added on December. As the third option.
Election winner might make the Parliament differ in power. and was remove in March 2025 due to server not having enough members to make it matter.
While said to be temporary and leader might choose to add it back, due to replacement of assembly i dont think this is the case.

And the final election was also done in march, and in april assembly was removed entirely, marking the end of its existence and leader voting.
From December 2023 to March 2025 it lasted 16 months before it got replaced with Shape mods.
